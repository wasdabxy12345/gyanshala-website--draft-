Welcome to Gyan Shala

We are a non-profit organization dedicated to providing quality education to underprivileged children.

Vision, Mission & Goal

Our Vision

To address schooling quality gaps and improve national social development.

Our Mission

Nationwide system solutions for quality school education without discrimination.

Our Goal

Learning levels for poor families comparable to elite schools.

Our Approach

Child-Centered

Focus on student capability over teacher capacity.

Integrated Solution

Combine teaching and curriculum design.

Outcome Driven

Measure learning through independent assessments.

Rigorous Quality Benchmarking

The program undergoes regular independent assessments by reputed third-party agencies such as EI, CfBT, DFID, and GMI.

These frequent, data-driven evaluations allow us to rigorously gauge the program's effectiveness and implement necessary changes to continuously improve student outcomes and maintain global standards.

Our Valued Donors

Support from organizations like SBI Foundation, Tata Trusts, and ICICI Foundation drives our impact.
