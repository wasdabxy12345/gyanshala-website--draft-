Welcome to Gyan Shala

We are a non-profit organization dedicated to providing quality education to underprivileged children by offering free educational resources, mentorship, and support programs.

Why we exists?

  • To develop a model for high quality schooling on a large scale for learners from modest social backgrounds, using available quality of teacher and financial resources.
  • To implement the model for groups of 10000-15000 children in 3-5 states, thus setting up state level demonstration and improvement centers in the area of school education to evolve effective state level curriculum and teaching solutions.
  • Work with State Government school system to introduce new model curriculum design and material to help improve learning outcomes.

Vision, Mission & Goal

What drives us, guides us, and defines our purpose

Our Vision

To become one of India’s most effective NGO programs addressing schooling quality gaps and contributing to improved national performance on social development indicators.

Our Mission
  • • To evolve a total system solution model for quality school education on a nationwide scale, without discrimination.
  • • To drive systemic transformation by improving pedagogy practices with high accountability and measurable learning outcomes.
  • • To implement programs at scale and generate credible evidence to inform government policy and program design.
Our Goal

To enable every child from poor urban and rural families to attain learning levels comparable to children studying in elite schools.

The 5A Implementation

We’ve adopted a 5A approach to counter the academic challenges keeping in mind the socio-economic barriers as follows:

Awareness

Behavioral Change towards education through continuous community engagement and motivation.

Availability & Accessibility

Equitable residential education held within the community (walking distance) with suitable timings.

Affordability

No indirect costs (books, transport, stationeries), removing barriers for poor families.

Accountability

Daily evaluations and external assessments guided by dedicated MEL staff for course correction.
